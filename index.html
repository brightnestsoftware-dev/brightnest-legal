Privacy Policy — English Dictation Trainer

Effective date: July 14, 2026  ·  App package: com.brightnest.dictationtrainer

In short: English Dictation Trainer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data — from children or adults. Everything the app does happens locally on your device, offline. There are no ads, no analytics, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

1. Who we are

English Dictation Trainer is published by Brightnest Software. The app helps parents run English spelling and dictation practice sessions with their children, using a built-in curriculum of pre-recorded words, images, and audio.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at brightnest.software@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

None. The app does not collect any personal information, usage data, device identifiers, location data, contact information, photos, audio recordings, or any other data — from adults or from children.

  • The app has no user accounts and no sign-up or login of any kind.
  • The app contains no analytics or crash-reporting SDKs (no Google Analytics, no Firebase Analytics, no Meta/Facebook SDK, or similar).
  • The app contains no advertising and no ad networks.
  • The app does not use the camera, microphone, or location, and does not request any such permissions.

3. Data stored on your device

Word lists you create, practice-session results, and progress statistics are stored only on your own device, in a local database. This data never leaves the device: there is no cloud backup, no syncing between devices, and no server operated by us. Deleting the app permanently deletes this data.

4. Internet access and in-app purchases

The app works fully offline. The only network permission it requests (INTERNET on Android) exists solely so that the platform's official billing system can process the optional one-time "Full Access" purchase and restore previous purchases.

Payments are handled entirely by Google Play Billing (on Android) or Apple's App Store / StoreKit (on iOS). We never see or store your payment details. Purchase processing is subject to Google's and Apple's own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

5. Children's privacy

The app is designed to be used by parents together with primary-school children, and we take children's privacy seriously. Because the app collects no data at all, no personal information from children is ever gathered, stored on our systems, or shared with anyone. This approach is intended to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and Google Play's Families Policy.

All in-app purchase entry points are directed at the parent, and purchases are protected by the device's platform-level purchase authentication (e.g., Google Play password or biometric confirmation, as configured by the parent).

6. Data sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties — because we do not collect any. No data is sold, rented, or transferred to any company or organization.

7. Security

Since no data is transmitted to us, the main security consideration is local storage. The app stores its data using standard local storage on your device, and the purchase status is stored using the platform's secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain).

8. Your choices

  • You can delete all app data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.
  • You can reset a list's cumulative statistics from within the app's Report screen.
  • No data deletion request to us is necessary, because we hold no data about you or your child.

9. Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data (for example, in a future version), we will update this page and revise the effective date above. Material changes will also be reflected in the app's store listing Data Safety section.
